Sign up

Find out when we are offering this workshop by visiting the library calendar , or request a custom instance for your course by emailing maps@harvard.edu .

Requirements

All you will need for this workshop are two free accounts. (1) Felt.com , our mapping visualization site, and (2) NHGIS.org , the site we use to access census data. Instructions for registering are included in the workshop steps.

Attribution

This workshop is inspired by a class project by Libby Wu ‘24. During Fall 2023, the Harvard Map Collection partnered with Tara Menon’s English course, City Fictions. Libby contributed her project data to help make GIS methods more accessible.
