A GIS database and webmap of historic Black life on Boston's Beacon Hill

Digital humanities and GIS project to facilitate discovery of resources depicting Black history in 18th and 19th century Boston.

November 22, 2022 1 min read

This is a prototype for Mapping Black Boston, a research project by David Weimer. Dave, our former Map Librarian, is now Curator of Maps and Director of the Center for the History of Cartography at the Newberry Library. For information on the project, or to collaborate, you can get in touch with Dave.

Check out the interactive Mapping Black Boston prototype.

To access the underlying project data, visit the project Github.
